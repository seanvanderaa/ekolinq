What if I need to reschedule?

After scheduling your pickup, you have the ability to edit the date via the "Edit a request" button in the top right corner of the page. In your confirmation email, you'll receive a unique number that you can then use to edit your request.

What time will you pick up my items?

All pick ups occur between 8am-4pm on the day you request.

Why should I use EkoLinq?

EkoLinq is heavily involved in our local ecosystem—whether through collaborations with other businesses, donating proceeds from our work to community members, or by providing a better outlet for textile waste, EkoLinq aims to have a positive impact in the Tri-Valley.
