Convenient Textile Drop-Off Now Available!

We’re excited to offer a new way to recycle your unwanted clothing and household textiles with EkoLinq. In addition to our regular door-side pickup service, we now have drop boxes available in Pleasanton—making it even easier to keep textiles out of landfills. Whether you choose drop-off or pickup, you’re helping make a difference in local sustainability.

Do you remember what items we accept?
See the full list here.

Located in the Rock Bible Church parking lot.

Accepted Items

  • Pants, jeans, skirts, dresses, suits, shorts, shirts
  • Coats, jackets, gloves, hats, scarves
  • Shoes, boots, heels, sneakers, sandals, socks
  • Bras, underwear, slips, camisoles, tights
  • Handbags, belts, ties, headbands
  • Towels, sheets, comforters, blankets, tablecloths
  • Wallets, totes, backpacks, stuffed animals

Unaccepted Items

  • Textiles that are wet, moldy, or contaminated with chemicals
  • Bio-hazardous waste
  • Mattresses, furniture, or other similar oversized items
